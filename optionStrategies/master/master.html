Master Option Strategies

Short Call

Setup

  • Sell a call, strike price A
  • Generally, the stock price will be below strike A

Short Put

Setup

  • Sell a put, strike price A
  • Generally, the stock price will be above strike A

Short Straddle

Setup

  • Sell a call, strike price A
  • Sell a put, strike price A
  • Generally, the stock price will be at strike A

Short Strangle

Setup

  • Sell a put, strike price A
  • Sell a call, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock price will be between strikes A and B

Synthetic Long

Setup

  • Buy a call, strike price A
  • Sell a put, strike price A
  • The stock should be at or very near strike A

Synthetic Short

Setup

  • Sell a call, strike price A
  • Buy a put, strike price A
  • The stock should be at or very near strike A

Front Spread With Calls

Setup

  • Buy a call, strike price A
  • Sell two calls, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will be below or at strike A

Front Spread With Puts

Setup

  • Sell two puts, strike price A
  • Buy a put, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will be at or above strike B.

Double Diagonal

Setup

  • Buy an out-of-the-money put, strike price A (Approx. 60 days from expiration - "back-month")
  • Sell an out-of-the-money put, strike price B (Approx. 30 days from expiration - "front-month")
  • Sell an out-of-the-money call, strike price C (Approx. 30 days from expiration - "front-month")
  • Buy an out-of-the-money call, strike price D (Approx. 60 days from expiration - "back-month")
  • Generally, the stock price will be between strike price B and strike price C.

Jade Lizard

Setup

  • Sell a put, strike price A
  • Sell a call, strike price B
  • Buy a call, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will between strike A and C
