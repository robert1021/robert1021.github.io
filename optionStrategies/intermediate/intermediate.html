Intermediate Option Strategies

Long Call

Setup

  • Buy a call, strike price A
  • Generally, the stock price will be at or above strike A

Long Put

Setup

  • Buy a put, strike price A
  • Generally, the stock price will be at or below strike A

Long Call Spread

Setup

  • Buy a call, strike price A
  • Sell a call, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will be at or above strike A and below strike B

Long Put Spread

Setup

  • Sell a put, strike price A
  • Buy a put, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will be at or below strike B and above strike A

Poor Mans Covered Call

Setup

  • Buy an in-the-money LEAPS call, strike price A
  • Sell an out-of-the-money short-term call, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock price will be closer to strike B than strike A
