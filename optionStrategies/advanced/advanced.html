Advanced Option Strategies

Short Call Spread

Setup

  • Sell a call, strike price A
  • Buy a call, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will be below strike A

Short Put Spread

Setup

  • Buy a put, strike price A
  • Sell a put, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will be above strike B

Long Straddle

Setup

  • Buy a call, strike price A
  • Buy a put, strike price A
  • Generally, the stock price will be at strike A

Long Strangle

Setup

  • Buy a put, strike price A
  • Buy a call, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock price will be between strikes A and B

Back Spread With Calls

Setup

  • Sell a call, strike price A
  • Buy two calls, strike price B
  • Generally, the stock will be at or around strike price A

Back Spread With Puts

Setup

  • Sell a put, strike price B
  • Buy two puts, strike price A
  • Generally, the stock will be at or around strike price B

Long Calendar Spread With Calls

Setup

  • Sell a call, strike price A (near-term expiration - "front-month")
  • Buy a call, strike price A (with expiration one month later - "back-month")
  • Generally, the stock will be at or around strike A

Long Calendar Spread With Puts

Setup

  • Sell a put, strike price A (near-term expiration - "front-month")
  • Buy a put, strike price A (with expiration one month later - "back-month")
  • Generally, the stock will be at or around strike A

Diagonal Spread With Calls

Setup

  • Sell an out-of-the-money call, strike price A (approx. 30 days from expiration - "front-month")
  • Buy a further out-of-the-money call, strike price B (approx. 60 days from expiration - "back-month")
  • At expiration of the front-month call, sell another call with strike A and the same expiration as the back-month call
  • Generally, the stock will be below strike A

Diagonal Spread With Puts

Setup

  • Sell an out-of-the-money put, strike price B (near-term expiration - "front-month")
  • Buy a further out-of-the-money put, strike price A (with expiration one month later - "back-month")
  • At expiration of the front-month put, sell another put with strike B and the same expiration as the back-month put
  • Generally, the stock will be above strike B

Iron Butterfly

Setup

  • Buy a put, strike price A
  • Sell a put, strike price B
  • Sell a call, strike price B
  • Buy a call, strike price C
  • Generally, the stock will be at strike B

Broken Wing Butterfly With Calls

Setup

  • Buy a call, strike price A
  • Sell two calls, strike price B
  • Skip over strike price C
  • Buy a call, strike price D
  • Generally, the stock will be at or below strike A

Broken Wing Butterfly With Puts

Setup

  • Buy a put, strike price A
  • Skip strike price B
  • Sell two puts, strike price C
  • Buy a put, strike price D
  • Generally, the stock will be at or above strike D

Inverse Broken Wing Butterfly With Calls

Setup

  • Sell a call with strike price A
  • Buy two calls with strike price B
  • Skip over strike price C
  • Sell a call with strike price D
  • Generally, the stock will be at or around strike price A

Inverse Broken Wing Butterfly With Puts

Setup

  • Sell a put with strike price D
  • Buy two puts with strike price C
  • Skip over strike price B
  • Sell a put with strike price A
  • Generally, the stock will be at or around strike price D
